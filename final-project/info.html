July 1, 1979.

The day music stopped standing still.


Experience the tactile click, the analog warmth, and the device that changed how the world listens forever.


FEATURES BREAKDOWN

  • Stereo Sound: Despite its size, the TPS-L2 delivered true stereo separation that rivaled home distinct setups.
  • The "Guys & Dolls" Jacks: Music is better shared. The TPS-L2 features two headphone jacks (labeled A and B), allowing two people to plug in and listen simultaneously.
  • The Hotline Button: Need to speak to your friend? Press the orange "Hotline" button. It activates a built-in microphone and lowers the volume, layering the outside world over your music.
  • Tactile Perfection: Feel the satisfying mechanical clunk of the "Play" button and the smooth resistance of the sliding volume faders.

Long Live Analog. Whether you are a collector, an audiophile, or a nostalgic dreamer, the TPS-L2 reminds us that sometimes, you have to rewind to move forward.
